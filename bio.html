A Bit About Me

I am a freshman at Tufts University intending to major in Computer Science and French. I'm passionate about education and design, and within Computer Science, I'm interested in EdTech, front-end development, UX/UI, and Computer Science Education.

My CS journey began with an ill-fated One Direction fan blog that I ran on Tumblr in middle school. I started teaching myself basic HTML and CSS to change the layout of the blog, eventually becoming comfortable enough with the syntax to design my own themes. Today, my love of boy bands has (thankfully) faded, but the coding skills I learned through making themes have not, and I now use them to design and sell commissioned themes to individual bloggers. The love of coding and appreciation for design that I discovered on Tumblr eventually inspired me to enroll in the Computer Science program at Tufts.

On campus, I'm a Health Educator with the Peer Health Exchange, and have recently joined the CodeSquad JumboCode team to work on Frontend Development and UX/UI. This semester, I will also be volunteering with Tufts Girls of Code and leading the team to build their website.

Outside of school, I love skiing, baking brownies, hiking, and doing arts and crafts projects. I enjoy working with younger students, and in addition to my current work in Health education, I have experience working with students from all backgrounds as a tutor, mentor, and camp counselor.
